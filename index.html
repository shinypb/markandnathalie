Venue

On October 31, 2009, Mark and Nathalie met in Mountain View and drove to Alice’s Restaurant on Highway 35 for their first date. They split the bill.

On October 12, 2013, they will be married across the street at Mountain Terrace. It is an indoor/outdoor location with a grassy lawn, redwood deck, and a rustic lodge.

Ceremony + Reception

The ceremony and the reception will take place at the same venue, Mountain Terrace at Woodside. It is an indoor/outdoor location with a grassy lawn, redwood deck, and a rustic lodge.

Weather permitting, the ceremony will take place on the lawn. It will be non-religious, with family friend Jane Manning Hyatt officiating.

After the ceremony, there will be a short cocktail break with refreshments, and lawn games.

The reception will be a sit-down dinner on the deck with family toasts, cake-cutting, and dancing in the lodge to follow.

It is therefore with great attention to the curious and confused out there that we overburden you with facts, suggestions, and tips about our wedding to ensure that you feel welcome, comfortable, and unstressed.

What should I wear?

The recommended dress code is “dressy cocktail.” Dinner jackets, ties, and bowties are encouraged. A tux would be a step too far in one direction; jeans would be a step too far in the other direction. Dresses and skirts don’t need to be floor-length. Use your best judgment.

It is ABSOLUTELY ESSENTIAL that you dress for the weather, and in layers! October can be chilly in California, especially at a higher altitude. Please, please, please dress sensibly for a comfortable evening.

What kind of shoes should/shouldn't I wear?

The venue features a big grassy lawn, which you will almost certainly have to walk on. Fancy, spiky heels and suede boots are not good friends with nature.

Are there any colors that guests should avoid wearing?

The usual wedding tradition of saving white for the bride is a hopeful request. The wedding party will be dressed in purples and grays, although that doesn’t mean you should avoid these colors.

Are kids welcome?

Guests under 18 are admitted by invitation only. To ensure your kid has a good time, it is essential that you ask in advance. We can recommend some awesome babysitters.

Can I bring a date?

Unless your date is invited by name, no. Due to space limitations, we regret that will are having no +1s. Our wedding planner, Jessica, is authorized to exercise extreme discretion when enforcing this rule. Don’t mess with her!
